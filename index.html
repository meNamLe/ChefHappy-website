Our Mission

Using machine learning to detect multiple ingredients in a single photo, it provides quick and easy recipes using just the ingredients in your fridge.

Ease of Use.

People with interests in cooking might become unmotivated because they must source out their ingredients at their supermarket. With Chefhappy they can cook with what they already have.

User-Friendly.

All it takes to generate recipes is the simple click of a camera on their phone. A user can then like and dislike the recipes generated for future uses.

Powerful.

Utilize machine learning to recognize the food captured in the picture. By feeding known foods to the software, it can predict what the ingredient is accurately.
